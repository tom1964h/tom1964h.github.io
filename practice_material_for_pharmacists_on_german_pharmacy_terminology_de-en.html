German-language practice material for pharmacists – a learning path - 2018

Hey!
I worked in Germany as a pharmacist for more than two and a half years. In the meantime, I read popular scientific articles on many areas of pharmaceuticals. After my return to Hungary, I organized this collection into a readable format. Thus, nearly 200 lessons contain many references in German, which may be useful for non-German speaking pharmacists. The material is supplemented by a trilingual herbal list (German-Latin-Hungarian). (See Practice_Material_..._DE-EN.pdf).
